Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Krishna Tech World collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our app available on the Google Play Store.

This policy applies to all users of the app.

The app may collect personal information such as name, email address, age, gender, location, device information, and other data necessary for providing the services offered. We may collect this information when users register, fill out forms, or interact with the app features.

Additionally, non-personal information like device type, operating system, app usage statistics, and cookies may be collected automatically for analytics and app improvement purposes.

Krishna Tech World may use collected information for:

  • Providing personalized content and services
  • Improving app functionality and user experience

  • Communicating updates, offers, and promotions
  • Analyzing app usage and trends
  • Complying with legal requirements

Data Sharing

Krishna Tech World may share collected information in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers to assist us in operating the app and providing services to users. These third parties are contractually obligated to keep information confidential and only use it for the purposes specified by us.

  • In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction.
  • With user consent or as otherwise disclosed at the time of data collection.

Security Measures

Krishna Tech World takes the security of user information seriously and implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Restricted access to user information to authorized personnel only
  • Secure storage and transmission protocols

Despite these efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. Users acknowledge that the security of information cannot be guaranteed.

User Rights

Users have certain rights regarding their personal information collected by the app, including:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of their data
  • The right to rectify inaccurate or incomplete information
  • The right to request deletion of their data, subject to legal obligations

  • The right to withdraw consent for processing their data

To exercise these rights or for any inquiries regarding their data, users can contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any changes. By continuing to use the app, you acknowledge and agree to the updated policy.
